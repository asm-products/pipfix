What is PipFix?
When you search a movie on IMDB you get an average of all the users' votes. This average tends to be useless because it has nothing to do with you and/or your interests. On PipFix what you get will be only the average of people that you follow. Using this weighted average the service will give you a collection of useful information based on your followed users input.
It's as easy as 1, 2, 3
1
Make an account and follow users with common interests
2
Vote on movies you are intersted in to improve your followers results
3
Enjoy your personalized results
